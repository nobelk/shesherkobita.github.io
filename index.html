Shesher Kobita

serenity, thoughtful comfort, & modern care

Shesher Kobita is envisioned as an intimate retreat for reflection, rest, and reconnection—framed by courtyards, books, gardens, prayer access, and practical proximity to Dhaka and modern geriatric healthcare.

Why Shesher Kobita?

Everything needed for a comfortable, cultured, and restorative stay

  • Modern Healthcare - Access to modern healthcare remains part of the concept’s reassuring location story.
  • Organic Food - Fresh, home-style meals centered on seasonal produce and gentle flavors acquired from nearby organic farms.
  • Cozy community living - tranquil environment surrounded by trees, rivers, and vast rice fields
  • Mosques - several mosques in walking distance
  • Effective Management - managed by professional who are experts in elder and geriatric care.
  • Near Dhaka - Close enough for family visits, weekend stays, and practical convenience.

Concept Design

The Shesher Kobita concept pairs open-air village stillness with a refined hospitality experience. The architecture centers around a courtyard so each room feels connected to sunlight, conversation, and gardens while maintaining a gentle sense of privacy.

Its visual language draws directly from the logo—layered greens, crisp white space, and deep charcoal accents—to create an atmosphere that feels restorative, elegant, and easy to inhabit.

Design Concept

The architectural study highlights planning intent—verandas, the open courtyard, guest circulation, and a calm progression from room to room.

A Village Home

Greenery, open sky, filtered light, and a slower pace shape a deeply restful atmosphere.

Functional Living

A welcoming arrival area, accessible cafeteria and flexible shared spaces support family visits and day-to-day comfort.

Active Living

Reading, reflective walks, courtyard gatherings, gardening moments, and peaceful creative time fill the day gently.
